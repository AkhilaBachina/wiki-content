FlexiAF deployment process in Charm

From SugarWiki
Jump to: navigation, search

This presents our best undestanding of FlexiTAF deployment in Charm release. (Based on discussion with Flexman.)

  • TFRM will control the compilation and packaging of FlexiTAF (currently done with Maven)
  • TFRM will produce the package (e.g. FlexiTAF.tar) to e.g. /flexman/builds/FlexiTAF -dir
    • flexman will receive information of this (automatic email from maven or some more advanced solution to be decided)
  • FlexiPlatform (FlexiTAF) customers will get information of the new version of our product (i.e. FlexiTAF) from FlexiPlatform build reports generated by flexman
    • customers can then download the package from e.g. /flexman/builds/FlexiTAF -dir

Exact procedure and e.g. the directory name will be decided later. This procedure will provide us the freedom to manage our packages in most suitable manner for us. (currently Maven + SVN)
