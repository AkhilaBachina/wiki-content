TA Days Wiki pages Presentation materials

[edit] Day 1

  • Other products compared to FP has much stronger support from the management to the test automation
  • Some products have found it useful to assign responsible person for each testcase
  • IPA uses bug tracking for testcases
  • IPA offers test automation solution to the applications
  • Most of the test automation tools were developed inside the product
  • Testcase scripts should be considered in similar way as the rest of the SW
  • Common confusion for the term "high-level TA tool". Better term would be "NSN promoted TA tool".

[edit] Day 2

  • Presented tools
    • IPSL
      • Ex-siemens tool that has in-house support and development team
      • Runs on windows pc connected directly to SUT.
      • Test cases are different kinds of traffic simulations
      • Exports results to QualityCenter
    • TAS
      • Developed in TietoEnator
      • Web UI: test cases are presented as matrix where pre-defined elements of the test environment are in columns and time flow in rows. User clicks the cells and adds actions to individual elements or between the elements
    • BTAF
      • Has very similar architecure with Pegasus (Eclipse RCP based)
      • 9 persons dedicated development team.
  • Used and developed internal tools/solutions need marketing inside the company. People don't just find them by themselves.
  • All presented test automation frameworks were quite similar. No superior ideas compared to FlexiTAF surfaced.
  • Model based testing and CASE-tools are pretty similar: they don't work in practice (or aren't worthwhile)in complex projects.
  • Many test framework projects have twice/thrice the amount of resource we are having
  • Many test framework projects have limited scope: they enable the testing of single product.
  • There aren't swiss army knife testing tool/framework. You always need different tools for different type of testing.
  • Some products had very minimal infrastructure for TWA. All products seems to develop their own structure for TWA without reusing any existing practice. But propably no one is marketing any TWA solutions.
  • People were promoting their solutions quite a lot, which propably increases the user base of the each promoted solution? Why didn't we do it?
  • Lot of different TA solutions in NSN. Quite many of these have been propably discussed in TAEG. Why no information have been shared to FP?
  • It was repeated in most presentations that strong program management commitment (incl. money and resources) is required to make automation happen in a product.
